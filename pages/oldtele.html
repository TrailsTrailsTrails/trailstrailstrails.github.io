Crossing 19: Nolans Brook

The 19th and last of all creek crossings of the Old Tele Track is infamous Nolans Brook. When we got there it was still possible to cross the creek. 15 cars later and the creek floor must have been washed away even more. While we had a snorkel we didn't realize that the air filter box had a hole which, once stuck in the now deeper creek, let the engine eagerly suck water...
We still made it to the Cape and back to Cairns before any repairs.

Overall Rating: 5/5 | Entry & Exit: 2/5 | Surface: 2/5 | Depth: 6/5

22 days Larapinta Trail Review Series

The Larapinta Trail is a 223 km long hiking trail in the Western MacDonnell Ranges near Alice Springs, NT in Outback Australia. I've hiked this trail over a total of 22 days but it is typically hiked in 10 - 20 days. I took my time because I enjoy exploring and being out here. This allowed me to hike many side trails which increased my walking distance to about 300 km.

Old Telegraph Track Review Series

The old Telegraph Track on the Cape York peninsula is an iconic 4x4 track in Australia's far north. The 116 km can only be accessed during the dry season and take 1-2 days. There are many creeks to cross and you will often have spectators at the more impressive crossings.
